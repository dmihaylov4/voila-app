## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I start a workout?
Tap the "+" button on the home screen and select exercises from the library.

### How do I track my progress?
View the Calendar tab to see your workout history and streaks.

### How do I sync with Apple Health?
Go to Profile → Connections → Enable HealthKit sync.

### The app crashes when I...
Please email us with:
- What you were doing when it crashed
- Your iOS version
- Screenshots if possible

We'll fix it ASAP!
