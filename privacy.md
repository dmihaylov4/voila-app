# Privacy Policy for VOLIA

**Last updated:** November 9, 2025

## Overview
VOLIA is committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect any data.** Period.

All workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. We have no access to this data.

## HealthKit Integration
If you choose to enable HealthKit synchronization:
- Workout data is shared with Apple Health on your device
- This data is managed entirely by Apple according to their privacy policy
- We do not have access to your HealthKit data
- You can revoke access at any time in iOS Settings

## Notifications
If you enable notifications:
- All reminders are generated locally on your device
- No notification data is sent to external servers

## Third-Party Services
VOLIA does not use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks  
- Tracking technologies
- Cloud storage services

## Children's Privacy
VOLIA does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
